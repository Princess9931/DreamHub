1
10वीं (Matriculation / SSC)
भारत में शिक्षा की पहली महत्वपूर्ण परीक्षा

परीक्षा बोर्ड

CBSE / ICSE / State Board

उम्र

14–16 साल

मुख्य विषय

Math, Science, SST, Hindi, English

पासिंग मार्क्स

33% (Board अनुसार)

10वीं के बाद क्या करें?
  • 11वीं-12वीं (Science/Commerce/Arts) — अगर आगे पढ़ना है
  • ITI (Industrial Training Institute) — 2 साल का vocational course, जल्दी नौकरी
  • Polytechnic Diploma — 3 साल, Engineering diploma
  • NDA (National Defence Academy) — 10वीं बाद Army, Navy, Air Force
10वीं बाद सरकारी नौकरी के अवसर

Railway (रेलवे) भर्ती

  • RRB Group D — Track Maintainer, Helper (10वीं पास)
  • RRB NTPC (Matric Level) — Junior Clerk, Goods Guard
  • वेतन: ₹18,000 – ₹35,000/माह

SSC (Staff Selection Commission)

  • SSC MTS (Multi Tasking Staff) — Central Govt. offices
  • SSC CHSL (10+2 के बाद, लेकिन अभी तैयारी शुरू करें)
  • वेतन: ₹18,000 – ₹25,000/माह

Indian Army / Police

  • Indian Army Soldier GD — 10वीं के बाद (17.5-21 साल)
  • State Police Constable — 10वीं/12वीं
  • CISF, BSF, CRPF Constable भर्ती
💡 Tip: 10वीं में 60%+ marks लाने की कोशिश करें — बाद की परीक्षाओं में eligibility आसान होती है।

12वीं बाद सरकारी नौकरी

SSC CHSL (Combined Higher Secondary Level)

  • Lower Division Clerk (LDC), Data Entry Operator (DEO)
  • Postal Assistant, Sorting Assistant
  • वेतन: ₹19,900 – ₹63,200/माह
  • परीक्षा: Tier-1 (CBT) + Tier-2 (Descriptive)

Banking — IBPS Clerk

  • Public sector banks में Clerk भर्ती
  • SBI Clerk, PNB, BOB, Canara Bank
  • वेतन: ₹11,765 – ₹31,540 + Allowances

Indian Air Force / Navy

  • Airmen (Group X & Y) — 12वीं Science/Arts
  • Indian Navy Sailor MR/NMR
  • Coast Guard Navik GD & DB

State Government Jobs

  • Patwari / Lekhpal (Revenue Dept.)
  • Anganwadi Worker, ASHA Worker
  • State Police Constable, Home Guard
3
Diploma / ITI (Vocational Courses)
जल्दी नौकरी पाने का सबसे अच्छा रास्ता
ITI (Industrial Training Institute)

अवधि

6 महीने – 2 साल

पात्रता

8वीं / 10वीं पास

  • Electrician (NSQF Level 5)
  • Fitter, Turner, Welder
  • Computer Operator (COPA)
  • Mechanic (Motor Vehicle)
  • Electronics Mechanic
  • Plumber, Mason, Carpenter
Polytechnic Diploma

अवधि

3 साल (10वीं बाद)

पात्रता

10वीं Math & Science

  • Civil Engineering
  • Mechanical Engineering
  • Electrical Engineering
  • Computer Science
  • Electronics & Communication
  • Chemical Engineering
ITI/Diploma धारकों के लिए सरकारी नौकरी

Railways (RRB ALP & Technician)

  • Assistant Loco Pilot (ALP) — ITI Electrician/Fitter/Mechanic
  • Junior Engineer (JE) — Diploma holders
  • Technician Grade-3 — विभिन्न trades
  • वेतन: ₹19,900 – ₹63,200/माह + allowances

PSUs (Public Sector Undertakings)

  • BHEL, SAIL, NTPC, GAIL — ITI Apprentice/Technician
  • ONGC, HPCL, BPCL — Diploma Engineer Trainee
  • DRDO, ISRO — Junior Research Fellow (Diploma)
  • वेतन: ₹21,000 – ₹55,000/माह

State PWD / CPWD

  • Junior Engineer (JE) Civil/Electrical — Diploma
  • Technical Assistant — Diploma in relevant trade
  • Municipal Corporation Technical Posts
💡 Lateral Entry: Diploma के बाद B.Tech 2nd year में सीधे admission ले सकते हैं (Lateral Entry Scheme)!

Graduation के बाद सरकारी नौकरी

SSC CGL (Combined Graduate Level) — सबसे बड़ी परीक्षा

  • Inspector of Income Tax, Inspector CBI, Assistant Audit Officer
  • Junior Statistical Officer (JSO)
  • Sub Inspector in CBI, NIA
  • वेतन: ₹25,500 – ₹1,51,100/माह (Post अनुसार)

Banking — IBPS PO / SBI PO

  • Probationary Officer (PO) in Nationalized Banks
  • RBI Assistant, RBI Grade B Officer
  • NABARD Development Assistant
  • वेतन: ₹52,000 – ₹95,000/माह (PO)

UPSC CDS (Defence Services)

  • IMA (Army Officer) — Any Graduation
  • AFA (Air Force Officer) — Graduation with Physics/Math
  • OTA (Officer Training Academy)
  • वेतन: ₹56,100/माह (Lieutenant) + भत्ते

State PSC (State Civil Services)

  • Deputy Collector, DSP, BDO — State level IAS जैसा
  • Sub-Inspector of Police (State)
  • Naib Tehsildar, Block Development Officer

Teaching (TGT/PGT)

  • Trained Graduate Teacher (TGT) — B.Ed + Graduation
  • CTET / State TET पास करके सरकारी School में teacher
  • KVS, NVS Teacher — Central Schools
  • वेतन: ₹35,000 – ₹70,000/माह

PG के बाद Top सरकारी नौकरी

UPSC IAS/IPS/IFS (Civil Services Examination)

  • IAS — District Collector, Secretary to Govt.
  • IPS — Superintendent of Police, IB, RAW
  • IFS — Indian Foreign Service, Embassy postings
  • वेतन: ₹56,100 – ₹2,50,000/माह + perks

UPSC IES / ESE (Engineering Services)

  • M.Tech/B.Tech वाले दे सकते हैं
  • Railways, CPWD, BRO, MES में Class-1 Officer
  • DRDO Scientist — GATE + Interview
  • वेतन: ₹56,100 – ₹1,77,500/माह

RBI Grade B Officer

  • Graduation 60% + PG preferred
  • Phase 1, 2, Interview
  • वेतन: ₹55,000 + allowances = ~₹1 Lakh/माह CTC

University / College Teaching (Assistant Professor)

  • UGC-NET / SLET पास करना जरूरी
  • Central University, IIT, NIT में faculty
  • वेतन: ₹57,700 – ₹1,44,200/माह (7th Pay Commission)

PhD + Research (CSIR/ICMR/DST)

  • CSIR JRF/SRF — ₹31,000 – ₹35,000/माह Fellowship
  • DRDO Research — Defence research labs
  • ISRO Scientist — Space research (SC/SD)
